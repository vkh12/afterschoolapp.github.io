About Us

Established in 2021, AfterSchool was founded by a group of students who experienced difficulty finding opportunities and meeting new people from a remote setting (during a global pandemic).

AfterSchool aims to provide students with a social networking platform to bridge the gap between campus opportunities and students. This app promotes time management, organization, and cohesive communication from club coordinators to students, reinforcing the college experience.
